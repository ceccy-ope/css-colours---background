5 ways to motivate yourself

When we leave this world, we will ask ourselves one question: What’s different because I was here? And the answer to that question will be the difference that we made.The actions we took.

Action will be all that matters.We could have made a difference every hour, everyday, if we had wanted to.

So how do we do that? How do we motivate ourselves to get into action? How do we live a life of action and difference-making? I share 5 tips below.

Get on your death bed

Clearly imagine yourself lying on your own death bed, and fully realize the feelings connected with dying and saying good-bye.

Then mentally invite the people in your life who are important to you to visit your bedside, one at a time.

As you visualize each friend and relative coming in to visit you, speak to them loudly, telling them what you want them to know as you are dying.

Stay Hungry

Create a vision of who you want to be, and then live into that picture as if it were already true.

Have something to wake up for in the morning. Something that you are “up to” in life so that you will stay hungry.

Create that vision right now. Don’t live a moment longer without one. Watch what being hungry to live that vision does to your ability to motivate yourself.

Tell Yourself A True Lie

By "lying” to yourself, you are creating a vision of who you want to be.

Many people are unable to see the truth of who they could be. An easy way is to express it as a fantasy

Without a picture of your highest self, you can’t live into that self. Fake it till you make it. The lie will become the truth.

Keep Youe Eyes On The Prize

Focus is everything. "The team that is more focused today is the team that will win the game.”

Many tend to lose focus in life because they’re perpetually worried about so many negative possibilities

When you focus on what you want, it will come into your life. When you focus on being a happy and mo- tivated person, that is who you will be.

Simplify Your Life

One of the benefits of creatively planning your life is that it allows you to simplify.

You can weed out, delegate, and eliminate all activities that don’t contribute to your projected goals.

fuse all small tasks together and make the doing of them one task so that you can have more time for yourself.
